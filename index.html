Welcome to Cougar!

So what is Cougar?

Cougar is a company founded in 2023 with the intent on creating unique experiences for our clients who have the desire to meet and see dangerous wild cats and animals up close and personal. With our trained professionals you can schedule a tour at one of our state of the art facilities where you can meet and play with the cubs of some of the most dangerous animals in the world!

Why start Cougar?

We found that most private zoos and facilities of the sort are not maintained properly and that many of the animals are maltreated to expolit them for profit. Our company is run as non-profit, and all the income goes 100% back into the maintaining habitats and hiring professionals to oversee operations. We pride ourselves in allowing guests the unique experience of getting to play with the cubs of these animals, while making sure they follow precautions and treat the animals with upmost respect and care.
